The Distributor shall facilitate the following services through POS/VLE to its registered LPG customers:

  • Ujjwala category
  • General category

Supply and distribution of LPG cylinders up to 100 kg through POS/VLE

‚öôÔ∏è 2. Functional and Operational Modalities

Cylinder Storage Requirements

100 kg

Maximum Storage Capacity at VLE Premises

  1. POS/VLE shall install a Cylinder Storage Cage, designed as per PESO standards, for safe storage of up to 100 kg of LPG in cylinders.
  2. The POS/VLE shall not store more than 100 kg LPG at any given time.
  3. An initial stock of around seven 14.2 kg cylinders will be supplied by the Distributor and accounted as inventory at the Distributor's go-down.

Note: CSC will bear the one-time cost for cylinder cage storage.

Equipment & Training

Pre-delivery Check Equipment

Weighing scale, O-ring tester, leak detector shall be provided by the Distributor on a returnable basis.

Training

Training for POS/VLE and staff shall be provided by the Distributor.

PESO-compliant cylinder storage cage (max 100 kg capacity)
