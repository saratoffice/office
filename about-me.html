Professional Journey

My career in public service and civil supplies

Civil Supply Office, Bolangir

Assistant Civil Supplies Officer
December 2020 - Present

Managing essential commodities distribution, ensuring fair price shops operation, implementing government schemes, and monitoring supply chain efficiency in the district.

Leading a team of 15+ staff members and coordinating with 200+ fair price shops across Bolangir district.

Odisha State Civil Supplies Office

Incharge - Multiple Blocks
2019 - 2020

Served as Incharge in Aska Block, Dharakote Block, and supervised essential commodities distribution across multiple blocks in Ganjam district.

Implemented digital tracking systems for supply chain management and reduced distribution delays by 40%.

Food Supplies & Consumer Welfare Department

Inspector of Supplies
2018 - 2019

Worked as Inspector of Supplies in Sheragada Block, Surada Block, and FSCW District Head Office in Chatrapur, ensuring compliance and efficient distribution systems.

Conducted regular inspections of 150+ fair price shops and resolved 500+ consumer complaints during tenure.
