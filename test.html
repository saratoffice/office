Work is God

"Instead of plucking flowers to adorn transient spaces, let us cherish them in their sanctified place of growth."

For proper reverence lies not in their brief adornment of our assets but in the nurturing care that allows them to flourish in the quiet dignity of nature's embrace.

"Work is God"

True devotion, in my view, is not confined to rituals or material entreaties but is rather expressed through our actions. "Work is God," for in every task we undertake, we invoke the divine energy. The labor we invest in life's duties is an offering to the cosmos, an alignment with the greater purpose of creation.

Therefore, it is through conscientious work, and not through ceremonial wishes, that we truly honor the divine presence in all its manifestations.

Professional Journey

My career in public service and civil supplies

2020 - Present

Civil Supply Office, Bolangir

Assistant Civil Supplies Officer
December 2020 - Present

Managing essential commodities distribution, ensuring fair price shops operation, implementing government schemes, and monitoring supply chain efficiency in the district. Leading a team of 15+ staff members and coordinating with 200+ fair price shops.

2019 - 2020

Odisha State Civil Supplies Office

Incharge - Multiple Blocks
2019 - 2020

Served as Incharge in Aska Block, Dharakote Block, and supervised essential commodities distribution across multiple blocks. Implemented digital tracking systems and reduced distribution delays by 40%.

2018 - 2019

Food Supplies & Consumer Welfare Department

Inspector of Supplies

Worked as Inspector of Supplies in Sheragada Block, Surada Block, and FSCW District Head Office. Conducted regular inspections of 150+ fair price shops and resolved 500+ consumer complaints.
