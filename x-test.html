Marginal Relief Analysis i

What is Marginal Relief?

When income is just above ₹12 lakh, tax is capped at (Income − ₹12,00,000) so you never pay more tax than your extra income.


Example (₹12,10,000 income):
Normal tax: ₹61,500 → Relief tax: ₹10,000 only

Applicable for income: ₹12,00,001 – ₹12,75,000
